Defining Patient Access Readiness

Patientaccessreadinessistheconditioninwhich apatient andthe supporting organizationhave completed,verified,communicated,andgovernedtheessentialstepsrequiredforthepatientto receivecarewithoutavoidableadministrative,financial,informational,orworkflow-relateddisruption. Thisdefinitionintentionallyholdstwo sidestogether.Thepatientmustbe supported towardreadiness, but the organization must alsobereadyto supportthepatient.

Patient Access Readiness as a Governable Signal

Patient accessreadinessbecomespowerfulwhen it is understood as a signal.A signal isnot just afield in a database.It isa structuredrepresentation of meaningthathelps anorganization decidewhat todonext.In patient access,thereadiness signal asks:Is thispatientreadyforthenext stepincare,and ifnot,whatmust happenbeforereadinesscanbe achieved?
